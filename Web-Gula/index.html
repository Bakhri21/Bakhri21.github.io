Coconut Palm Sugar

"Palm Sugar" or "Coconut Sugar" is a type of natural sugar produced from the nectar of flowers or sap of coconut or palm trees. It has a distinct sweet taste with caramel undertones and is typically available in crystal, block, or syrup form. Palm sugar contains nutrients such as iron, calcium, and magnesium, and it has a low glycemic index, making it a healthier alternative to regular white sugar.
